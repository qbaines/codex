# Digital Series Script: Episode 3 – Britney Spears vs. the Paparazzi

**Opening Shot**: Quick montage of early 2000s pop culture—flashing cameras and Britney on stage.

**On‑screen text**: "February 2007: Britney lashes out at the paparazzi."

**Narrator**: "Few understood the toll this constant harassment took on her mental health. Britney later described feeling trapped with no escape from the flashbulbs."

**Cut to**: Clip of Britney speaking about the incident or a paraphrased quote if no rights.

**Narrator**: "Looking back, many see that moment as a desperate plea for privacy rather than a breakdown. It highlighted the darker side of fame in the mid‑2000s."
