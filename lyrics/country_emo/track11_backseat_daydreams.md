# Track 11 - Backseat Daydreams (Country Emo)

**Verse 1**
Dusty wheels rolling through a twilight haze,
Backseat reflections on my wandering days.
Tunes from the radio paint old scenes,
I'm chasing horizons and faded dreams.

**Chorus**
Backseat daydreams sing me to sleep,
Country emo stories run dark and deep.
Every mile scribbles new lines to keep,
Sharp-pen verses that slice while they weep.

**Verse 2**
Rest-stop coffee warms my restless hands,
Watching the fields pass like drifting sands.
Every sunset whispers where I belong,
Finding new strength in this traveling song.
