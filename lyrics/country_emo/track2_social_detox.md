# Track 2 - Social Detox (Scroll & Roll) (Country Emo)

**Verse 1**
Scroll fatigue hits like a runaway train,
I toss my phone aside for the open plain.
Trading city smoke for the smell of rain,
My thumbs find peace from the endless strain.

**Chorus**
Scroll and roll away, let the silence bloom,
High-friendly vibes in my country room.
Laughing at the quiet, letting stories spin,
These detoxed days help the healing begin.

**Verse 2**
Barn boards creak while I hum a tune,
Filtering my life under a prairie moon.
Friends think I've vanished, but I'm just out of range,
Finding myself on this wide-open change.
