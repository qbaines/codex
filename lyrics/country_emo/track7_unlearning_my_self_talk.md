# Track 7 - Unlearning My Self-Talk (Country Emo)

Old doubts echo like a rusty gate,
I've been hauling them around like extra weight.

I pen these lines like a shot of truth,
Country emo courage from my head to my roots.

**Verse 2**
Silence stretches out across this field,
All those voices losing their shield.
Every note I sing is an honest start,
Carving new space in a battle-scarred heart.
