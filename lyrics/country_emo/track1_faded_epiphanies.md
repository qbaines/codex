# Track 1 - Faded Epiphanies (Country Emo)

**Verse 1**
Sunset spilling over dusty roads,
Acoustic strings hum while my story unfolds.
I left behind the chatter of the city lights,
Trading neon noise for these starry nights.

**Chorus**
Faded epiphanies ride the evening breeze,
Sharp words echo through the rustling trees.
I'm shedding old skins as the campfire glows,
Country tinged emotions where the wild wind blows.

**Verse 2**
Miles of memory in every lane,
Heartbreak sketches lines that no rain can drain.
I sing with the crickets, raw and sincere,
Every faded epiphany drawing me near.
