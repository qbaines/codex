# Track 8 - Temporary Ghosting (Country Emo)

Sometimes I disappear to the edge of town,
No signal in sight, no social countdown.
I'm clearing my head where the cornfields sway,

Temporary ghosting, just a short retreat,
Country wind carries me off my feet.
I'll come back stronger when the world goes quiet,
These high-friendly breaks keep my soul in riot.

**Verse 2**
Old friends worry I'm slipping away,
But I'm just recharging in a secret hideaway.
When I return, my words hit sharp and clean,
Country emo stories shaping every scene.
