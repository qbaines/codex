# Track 12 - Outro: Growing Pains & Gains (Country Emo)

**Verse 1**
I've walked through shadows, felt the sting of rain,
Every step forward carries a touch of pain.
But I keep on trekking down this winding road,
Country chords guiding the weight of my load.

**Chorus**
Growing pains echo in the open night,
Every lesson carved in the pale moonlight.
I trade old habits for fields of change,
Country emo strength in my vocal range.

**Outro**
As the campfire fades and the stars appear,
I shed old fears that once drew near.
These gains are heavy with stories told,
A sharp-pen future as the embers grow cold.
