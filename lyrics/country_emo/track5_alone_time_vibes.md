# Track 5 - Alone Time Vibes (Country Emo)

**Verse 1**
Cabin in the woods, just me and a song,
Echoes of heartbreak carry me along.
No city sirens, only wind through trees,
I breathe in the quiet, feel my mind at ease.

**Chorus**
Alone time vibes under starlit skies,
Sharp words spilling where my spirit lies.
Melodies rise from a steel-string slide,
Country emo solace I can no longer hide.

**Verse 2**
Fireflies circle, lighting up the dark,
I jot down confessions with a restless spark.
Every solitude moment sharpens my pen,
I find strength in silence again and again.
