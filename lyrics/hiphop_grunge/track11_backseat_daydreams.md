# Track 11 - Backseat Daydreams (Hip Hop Emo Grunge)

**Verse 1**
Cruising down alleys with the windows cracked,
Beats shake the frame, memories flashback.
Staring at the skyline from the backseat gloom,
My rhymes chase shadows through the engine's boom.

**Chorus**
Backseat daydreams roll on, bar by bar,
Every mile a memory, every verse a scar.
Grunge chords growl beneath the hip-hop beat,
I'm chasing clarity on this endless street.

**Verse 2**
Streetlights blur as I scribble lines,
Finding hidden truths in the exit signs.
Gas-station coffee keeps my verses awake,
Every backseat dream another risk to take.
