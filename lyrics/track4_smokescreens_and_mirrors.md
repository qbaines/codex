# Track 4 - Smokescreens & Mirrors

**Intro**
Can't see straight through all this haze,
I'm laughing at reflections in my own maze.

**Verse 1**
Fake smiles scrolling past me at the speed of light,
I'm chasing something real in this pixelated night.
Every post is polished, every angle's set,
But I'm tripping on the truth that I haven't met.

**Pre-Chorus**
So I wipe away the filters, clear the static screen,
Looking for the meaning hiding in between.

**Chorus**
Losing the haze in my rearview, seeing what's real in this light,
No more smokescreens and mirrors; I'm stepping into the night.
Dancing through the fog with my eyes open wide,
Finding my footing on the other side.

**Verse 2**
Thought I had the picture perfect, thought I knew the score,
Till I realized I'm hungry for something more.
Memes can't feed my soul, likes can't fill the void,
I'm tossing outdated filters like receipts I've long destroyed,
Gotta break the glass and let my mind avoid.

**Bridge**
A touch of emo heartbreak, a hip hop beat,
Folk guitar strumming underneath my feet.
Pull apart illusions, let the realness shine,
Breathing in the clarity that's been mine all the time.

**Chorus**
Losing the haze in my rearview, seeing what's real in this light,
No more smokescreens and mirrors; I'm stepping into the night.
Dancing through the fog with my eyes open wide,
Finding my footing on the other side.

**Outro**
Mirror shards falling like confetti in the dark,
I'm walking through the smoke, setting off my spark.
