# Track 11 - Backseat Daydreams

**Intro**
Cruising down the side streets, playlist on repeat,
Letting all the noise fade while I'm sinking in the seat.
Backseat's my throne, driver rolls his eyes as I rhyme,

**Verse 1**
I trace my fears on windows, they vanish with the breeze,
Julia's candid whispers put my tangled mind at ease.
Juice's mellow honesty keeps the vibe so chill,
I'm learning how to wander without standing still.

**Pre-Chorus**
Looking at the world go by, unlearning all the schemes,
Finding truth in idle time, drifting through my dreams.

**Chorus**
Backseat daydreams, rolling nowhere fast,
Laughing at the detours as the city scrolls past.
Skipping every small talk, tuned out with a grin,
I'll catch my destination when I figure where I've been.

**Verse 2**
The driver's telling stories, I'm half a mile away,
Thinking 'bout the friends I ghost, it's easier that way.
Folk guitar in headphones, hip hop in my head,
Building little bridges with the rhymes I've left unsaid.

**Bridge**
If you're wondering where I'm going, I'm wondering the same,
Every winding road is part of this ridiculous game.

**Chorus**
Backseat daydreams, rolling nowhere fast,
Laughing at the detours as the city scrolls past.
Skipping every small talk, tuned out with a grin,
I'll catch my destination when I figure where I've been.

**Outro**
So here I ride in circles, a poet in the back,
Gaining peace in silence as the headlights fade to black.
